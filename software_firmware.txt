
Microcontroller Firmware
------------------------

From Zero to main() is a series of excellent articles from MemFault describing topics around firmware stuff.

Demystifying Firmware Linker Scripts
 Excellent article describing the details of the linker script
 https://interrupt.memfault.com/blog/how-to-write-linker-scripts-for-firmware

Bare metal C
 Describes the process from reset to main. There is an analysis of the gcc tools such as objdump.
 https://interrupt.memfault.com/blog/zero-to-main-1

How to Write a Bootloader from Scratch
